Gawr Gura

Gawr Gura (がうる・ぐら) was a female English-speaking Virtual YouTuber associated with hololive, debuting in 2020 as part of hololive English first generation "-Myth-" alongside Ninomae Ina'nis, Takanashi Kiara, Watson Amelia and Mori Calliope.

In May 2025, she graduated from hololive. During a nearly five-year term with the agency, she set numerous records, ultimately reaching 4.71 million YouTube subscribers before her retirement. Gura was widely recognized as the #1 subscribed VTuber on YouTube worldwide, a position she held since she overtook industry pioneer Kizuna Ai in 2021.
